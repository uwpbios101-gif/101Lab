Required Materials

Enter the room prepared to complete the Lab in the time allotted. Please bring:

  • Lab Manual: Must be in a 3-ring binder.
  • Textbook: Campbell Biology (12th Edition).
  • Electronic Device(s): Required for lab work.
  • Writing Utensils: Colored pencils or crayons (No pens).

Daily Laboratory Flow

  1. Arrival: Put backpack/coat in the closet or side cabinets.
  2. Hygiene: Wash hands for at least 20-30 seconds.
  3. Preparation: Grab supplies for the day.
  4. Assessment: Take the Weekly Quiz.
  5. Work: Begin exercises for the day.
  6. Equipment: Check out your assigned microscope (no sharing). Set up with millimeter ruler on stage.

Note: Stop all activities at 4:20 PM (Tues/Thur) or 11:20 AM (Wed) to start Metric conversions.

Policies & Pre-Lab

Missed Labs

  • Contact Instructor ASAP: If life happens, email immediately.
  • Make-up Policy: You generally cannot make up missed labs.
  • Schedule: Try to schedule a meeting day during the same week (See pg 4 of Lab Manual).

Pre-Lab Work (Paramount!)

  • Answer as many Lab Manual questions as possible before arrival.
  • Attempt the week's worksheet prior to your lab session.

Grading & Exams

Written Quizzes

  • Total: 6 written quizzes.
  • Format: In Lab | 1 Attempt | 5 Minutes.
  • Content: 10 questions (10 points).
    • Includes Fill in the blanks, True/False, Multiple choice, etc.
    • 9 questions from the previous lab.
    • 2 questions from the current week's lab.
    • Includes 2 practical questions.

Exams

  • 1st Exam: Labs 1 through 5.
  • 2nd Exam: Labs 6 through 10.
  • Details: 125 Points | 125 Questions (40 practical). Typically 1 hour.
